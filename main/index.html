What is Financial Analysis?

Definition: Financial Analysis, as the name itself signifies, is a process of critically studying, reviewing and interpreting the information provided in the company's financial statements so as to understand the same and take better decisions regarding the firm's operations. Financial Analysis is used to evaluate the present and past financial position of the enterprise
